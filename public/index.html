Season Rules — AFL 2026
Reference these by number when discussing changes
GETTING STARTED
1.Each player starts with $10,000 to invest.
2.Share prices are based on each team's ladder position — 1st costs $6.12, 18th costs $1.00.
3.Prices update each week when the admin (Tyson) enters the new AFL ladder results.
TRADING WINDOWS & LOCKOUT
4.Each round has a trading window that opens when the admin advances the round and closes 30 minutes before the first game.
5.During the trading window, you can only see your own live portfolio. All other players' portfolios show a snapshot from the end of the previous round.
6.Once the deadline passes, trading locks automatically. All portfolios are revealed.
7.You can undo any trade made during the current trading window — shares and fees are fully reversed.
THE SEASON
8.The season runs for 10 rounds: Opening Round, then Round 1 through Round 9.
PORTFOLIO LIMITS
9.Max 25% of your total portfolio in any single team.
10.Max 50% of your portfolio value in cash.
HOLD PERIODS
11.All shares must be held for a minimum of 2 rounds before selling.
BROKERAGE
12.Each trade incurs a brokerage fee of 0.5% of your total portfolio value.
13.Each additional trade in the same round doubles the fee — 1st: 0.5%, 2nd: 1.0%, 3rd: 2.0%.
INTEREST & TAX
14.Top 4 teams pay weekly interest: 1st: 2%, 2nd: 1.5%, 3rd: 1%, 4th: 0.5%.
15.Interest only applies if the team has been in the top 4 consecutively.
16.The team finishing last (18th) incurs a weekly tax of 1%.
PLAYERS
17.There are 3 human players (Tyson, Jas, Sam) and 7 AI opponents.
